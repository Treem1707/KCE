ABOUT US

We ensure environmentally sound Business and Industrial Practices

Our purpose as Kwaramba & Chapfunya Engineering is to help businesses and industries meet Environmental standards and ESG goals in a cost-effective manner whilst satisfying their economic/production targets by providing a multi-disciplinary approach through our diversified team consisting of Environmental Engineers, Biologists, Social scientists and Economists.

Providing sustainable development in Zimbabwe's cities and towns through examining existing industrial enterprises/opportunities and then designing, planning and implementing cleaner production technology and solutions thereby creating long-term value to the Zimbabwean Economy
